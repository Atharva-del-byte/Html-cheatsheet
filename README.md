## 🧪 Sample: Arguing With Mom (Semantic Tags)

```html
<header>
  <h1>Mom's House</h1>
</header>

<nav>
  <ul>
    <li>Kitchen</li>
    <li>Living Room</li>
    <li>Chappal Section</li>
  </ul>
</nav>

<main>
  <article>
    <h2>Today's Fight</h2>
    <p>Why didn't you clean your room?</p>
  </article>
</main>

<footer>
  <p>Respect your mom or face the chappal.</p>
</footer>
